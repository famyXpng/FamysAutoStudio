Next-Gen Capabilities

Built from the ground up to bypass traditional input simulation limitations.

Auto Tap Engine

High-speed interval clicking with humanized jitter. Directly injects clicks into specific window handles, saving your real mouse.

FamyScript (.fmac)

A custom, lightweight scripting language designed explicitly for synchronous, non-intrusive target window automation.

Task Scheduler

Autonomous background watchers. Fire off scripts automatically when specific windows open, close, or at specific times.

Force System Tray

Click one button to forcefully hide any target application into your System Tray, saving critical GPU & CPU overhead.
